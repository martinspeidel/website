I am a specialist for cutting edge 2D motion graphics.

The process

  • Strategy

    Reviewing the current animations to see how they perform and explore the brand to understand its core values. Identifying the target audience and required assets for each channel.

  • Visual concept

    Creating moodboards, storyboards and styleframes. Experimenting with motion styles to define the look and feel. Researching music for rhythm and pacing.

  • Animation

    Bring typography to life, animate images and videos, create dynamic logo animations, design smooth transitions, add unique patterns and enhance UI elements.

  • Documentation

    Combining strategy, principles, animations, and templates so your team can consistently showcase your brand in motion.
