My Process

  • Strategy

    Reviewing the current animations to see how they perform and explore the brand to understand its core values. Identifying the target audience and required assets for each channel.

  • Visual concept

    Creating moodboards, storyboards and styleframes. Experimenting with motion styles to define the look and feel. Researching music for rhythm and pacing.

  • Animation

    Bringing typography to life, animating images and videos, creating dynamic logo animations, designing smooth transitions, adding unique patterns, and enhancing UI elements.

  • Documentation

    Combining strategy, principles, animations, and templates so your team can consistently showcase your brand in motion.

This is me.

I studied Design at the University of Applied Sciences Mainz and then moved to Hamburg. After working at various agencies, I went freelance in 2014. Since then, I've had the chance to work on numerous exciting projects and brands.

I offer strategy and motion design for corporate branding, case films, logo animations, motion graphic templates, custom scripting, and digital assets for web projects.


My main tools are Adobe After Effects, the Adobe Creative Suite, Maxon Cinema 4D, VS Code. Currently learning Rive & Cavalry.

I work 100% remotely, ensure fast and secure data exchange, quick response, and smooth collaboration.

I live on the edge of Hamburg, surrounded by nature. I've got two kids, enjoy playing piano, and taking walks in the beautiful countryside.
